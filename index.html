🎮 Practice Round: How to Play

Step 1 of 6

Step 1 — Your Mission

Manage two junco populations: Urban UCSD and Mt. Laguna. Help them survive, reproduce, and adapt.

Step 2 — Secret Objectives

Each team can privately reveal its objective. Keep it secret until the end. Objectives are randomized each new game.

Step 3 — Resources

Each habitat has Food, Energy, and Safety. Choices lock if they would push any resource below 0. If no choices are available, that population dies.

Step 4 — Choose Strategies

Strategies stay active until changed. Check the resource cost table near the buttons before changing a strategy.

Step 5 — Run the Generation

Click Run Next Generation. Read the condition popup, then check resources, mood, badges, and the quick summary.

Step 6 — What to Watch For

  • Did bold or shy birds increase?
  • Did allele frequency change?
  • Did CORT go up or down?
  • Are resources in danger?

Controlled Variables

  • same food access
